Who I am

A chef shaped
by curiosity.

I hold a Master's in Data Science & Analytics from Royal Holloway, University of London — a background that gives me a unique analytical edge in the kitchen, from precise recipe development to understanding flavour science.

But my true calling has always been food. Since 2021, I've worked my way through some incredible kitchens — from learning the fundamentals as a commis chef, to working alongside celebrity chef Giancarlo Caldesi at his AA Rosette restaurant in Bray.

I thrive under pressure, care deeply about hygiene and food safety, and believe the best dishes are born from both technique and heart. I'm flexible, confident, and always ready to grow.
